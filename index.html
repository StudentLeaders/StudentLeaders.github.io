About Us


Our Mission

Our mission is to bring together student leaders to coordinate on accomplishing initives that put students first. We strive to connect student leaders across our campus and the nation to tackle the major issues afflicting our students such as sky rocketing costs, student safety, and mental health. To make meaningful strives on these issues on the campus level we will connect students across student governing bodies, greek life, and professional organizations. On the regional, state, and national level we will connect leaders to exchange ideas, launch multilateral initiatives, and push for broader change.


We hope to bring together these student leaders to grow professionally together, to not only teach each other, but experience different opportunities for professional development.




Our Projects

Our organizations is the culmination of years of student advocacy by our precursor organizations the Student Justice Coalition and the Student Protection Union.

Student Justice Coalition

The Student Justice Coalition was founded in the wake of concerns by UIC students over the disenfranchisement of international students in student government elections. The SJC launched a months long investigation that uncovered that students in the non-degree seeking program, who are overwhelmingly international, able to vote for Student Government President and Vice President. This story appeared in publications such as WTTW and Nextshark.

Covid-19 Response

In the wake of the Covid-19 pandemic, members of our team came togher to ensure the university put students first in their response through the formation of campus coalitions like the Student Protection Union and the John Donners for Student Trustee campaign. Together we successfully advocated for a partial refund of some university fees and for a credit / no credit grading policy. Throughout this our petition adovacting for a partial tuition refund received over 10,000 signatures. These stories appeared in publications such as ABC 7 Chicago, WTTW, and the Higher Education Podcast.

Advocacy & Action Campaign

Most recently our members lead the successful Advocacy and Action campaign for UIC's Undergraduate Student Government resulting in the victory of now President Anshu Nidamanuri, Vice President Arthur Kasowski, Chief of Staff John Donners, and the rest of their executive board and slate of Representatives.

Solve Cross-Partisan Issues Together

We aim to advocate for issues that affect all students. These are issues like the rising cost of attendance, student mental health, campus safety, freedom of expression, and student representation.

Cost of Attendance

- The cost of tuition, fees, and remote courses

- Universities and elected officials must take a proactive approach to halting increases and actively reducing costs

Mental Health

- Many universities lack the resources allocated to ensure students receive care for their mental health.

- Finding solutions to fill in the gaps and policies to ensure students health is focused on just as much as their money.

Freedom of Expression

Too many universities actively repress students right to free speech and expression. Advocating to improve these standards and put rules on the books to protect them

Ensuring a space for student publications to grow and offer a diversity of opinion.
